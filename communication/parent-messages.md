## Communication Guidelines

### Tone
- Friendly and encouraging
- Clear and concise
- Professional but warm

### Timing
- Game reminders: Day before
- Schedule changes: ASAP
- Practice updates: Morning of

### Formatting
- Use emojis for visibility
- Keep to 1-2 short paragraphs
- Include key details (who, what, when, where)
