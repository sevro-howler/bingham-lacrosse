# Transition Drills

**Phase focus:** Teaching what transition means (moving from defense to offense or vice versa)

---

## Wheels to Goal ⭐ *FOR TONIGHT*

**Phase:** Transition
**Objective:** Understand that transition means "run fast from defense to offense"
**Duration:** 15 minutes
**Equipment:** 4-5 balls, 2 cones (one at each end of field)
**Group size:** 8-12 players

### Setup
- Two cones 40-50 yards apart (defensive end to offensive end)
- Players line up at defensive cone
- Balls ready at defensive cone

### I Do (3 min) - Coach Demonstrates
**Show the concept:**
- "After we get the ball on defense, we TRANSITION to offense. That means RUN FAST!"
- "Watch me show WHEELS!"
- Pick up ball at defensive cone
- Sprint FULL SPEED to offensive cone (exaggerate the speed!)
- "This is WHEELS - no slowing down!"
- "In a game, this is how we go from defense to scoring!"

**Key terms to emphasize:**
- **"WHEELS!"** - Go fast, don't slow down
- **"Transition!"** - Moving from one end to the other

### We Do (6 min) - Guided Practice
**Run together:**
1. All players line up at defensive cone
2. First 3 players grab balls
3. Coach: "On my whistle, show me WHEELS!"
4. Blow whistle, players sprint to offensive cone
5. Coach runs WITH them: "WHEELS! WHEELS! Keep going!"
6. Bring them back, next 3 players go
7. Everyone gets 3-4 reps running full speed

**Coaching points:**
- ONE main thing: "Don't slow down halfway!"
- Celebrate the speed: "That's WHEELS! Full speed!"
- Keep energy HIGH

### You Do (6 min) - Independent Practice
**Make it competitive:**
- Split into 2-3 relay teams
- First player sprints with ball from defensive to offensive cone
- Tag next teammate, they go back the other way
- Keep shouting "WHEELS!" while teammates run
- First team to finish wins

**Success criteria:**
- Are they running FAST? (Not worried about stick skills, just SPEED!)
- Do they understand transition = speed?

### Game Connection
"In a game, when we get the ball back on defense, we yell WHEELS and run fast to score before the other team can get back to defend!"

### Common Mistakes
- Slowing down halfway (remind: "WHEELS all the way to the end!")
- Jogging instead of sprinting (demo again: "THIS is wheels!" and sprint)

## Look Up Pass

**Phase:** Transition
**Objective:** Learn to look for open teammates while running in transition
**Duration:** 15 minutes
**Equipment:** 3-4 balls, 4-6 cones
**Group size:** 8-12 players

### Setup
- Create a lane with cones (about 10 yards wide, 40 yards long)
- 3 players per group: 1 ball carrier, 2 runners alongside

### I Do (3 min)
**Show the concept:**
- "In transition, we run fast BUT we also LOOK UP to find open teammates!"
- Demonstrate: sprint with ball, head up, find open player running alongside
- Make a soft pass to them
- "See? WHEELS + LOOK UP = fast transition passing!"

**Key terms:**
- **"Look Up!"** - Find the open player
- **"Soft Pass!"** - Don't throw it too hard while running

### We Do (6 min)
**Practice together:**
1. Groups of 3 running down the lane
2. Middle player has ball
3. All three yell "WHEELS!" and sprint
4. Middle player practices looking left/right
5. Coach calls "LEFT!" or "RIGHT!" and ball carrier passes that direction
6. Repeat, rotate who has ball

**Coaching points:**
- Keep head up while running
- Soft passes - we're running fast, easy passes work better

### You Do (6 min)
**Independent:**
- Groups of 3 run transition
- Ball carrier decides who to pass to (no coach calling it)
- Focus: did they look up? Did they find the open guy?

**Success criteria:**
- Are they keeping their heads up while running?
- Do they understand you can pass in transition?

### Game Connection
"In games, sometimes the defense is back and you can't just run all the way. LOOK UP and pass to the open teammate!"

### Common Mistakes
- Looking down at the ball (remind: "Look UP!")
- Throwing too hard (remind: "Soft pass!")

## Chase After Shot

**Phase:** Transition (from offense back to defense/ride)
**Objective:** After a shot, chase to the line to play defense or get the rebound
**Duration:** 15 minutes
**Equipment:** 1-2 goals, lots of balls
**Group size:** 8-12 players

### Setup
- Shooting line about 12-15 yards from goal
- Cones marking the "line" behind the goal (endline)
- Players line up to shoot

### I Do (3 min)
**Show the concept:**
- "Watch what happens after I shoot!"
- Take a shot on goal
- Immediately sprint toward the endline: "CHASE!"
- "In a game, after we shoot, we CHASE to get the rebound or play defense!"

**Key terms:**
- **"Chase!"** - Run to the line after shooting
- **"Get back!"** - Transition to defense

### We Do (6 min)
**Practice together:**
1. Players shoot one at a time
2. After each shot, coach yells "CHASE!"
3. Shooter sprints to the endline
4. Coach explains: "If we miss, maybe we get the rebound! If they get it, we play defense!"
5. Everyone gets multiple reps

**Coaching points:**
- Don't admire your shot - CHASE immediately!
- This is transition from offense to defense

### You Do (6 min)
**Add competition:**
- Shoot, then chase to collect the rebound
- First one to touch the ball after the shot gets a point
- Builds habit of chasing

**Success criteria:**
- Do they chase automatically after shooting?
- Understand this is the transition moment?

### Game Connection
"In games, the play isn't over when you shoot! CHASE the shot and either get the rebound or get ready to defend!"

### Common Mistakes
- Standing and watching the shot (remind: "CHASE!")
- Giving up if they miss (remind: "Rebounds win games!")
