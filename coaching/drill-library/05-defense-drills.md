# Defense Drills

**Phase focus:** Teaching what defense means (stopping the other team from scoring, protecting our goal)

---

## Man-Ball Defense ⭐ *FOR THURSDAY*

**Phase:** Defense
**Objective:** Learn the basic defensive assignments - who guards the ball carrier (ball), who guards the other players (man)
**Duration:** 15 minutes
**Equipment:** 1 goal, 1-2 balls, pinnies for offense/defense
**Group size:** 8-12 players

### Setup
- Goal at one end
- 3 offensive players with ball (pinnies)
- 3 defensive players (different color pinnies)
- Ball with one offensive player

### I Do (3 min) - Coach Demonstrates
**Show the concept:**
- Point to offensive player with ball: "He has BALL!"
- Point to yourself (defender): "I guard BALL - I stay on him tight!"
- Point to other offensive players: "They are MAN - other defenders guard them!"
- "See? One defender on BALL, others on MAN!"
- Demonstrate following the ball: "Ball moves? I move with it!"

**Key terms to emphasize:**
- **"Man-Ball!"** - Who's on ball, who's on man
- **"I got ball!"** - Call it out
- **"I got man!"** - Call it out

### We Do (6 min) - Guided Practice
**Walk through slowly:**
1. Set up 3v3 around the goal
2. Coach: "Who has the ball?" (players point)
3. Coach: "Who's guarding BALL?" (one defender steps up)
4. Coach: "Everyone else has MAN!" (other defenders raise hands)
5. Pass the ball to different offensive player
6. Coach: "BALL MOVED! New defender on ball!"
7. Defenders switch assignments
8. Repeat many times, slow at first

**Coaching points:**
- ONE main thing: Know if you have BALL or MAN!
- Call it out: "I got ball!" or "I got man!"
- When ball moves, assignments change

### You Do (6 min) - Independent Practice
**Live 3v3:**
- Offense passes the ball around
- Defense must call out "Ball!" or "Man!" constantly
- Coach stops play randomly: "Who has ball? Who has man?"
- Players must answer correctly
- Focus: are they communicating?

**Success criteria:**
- Can they identify ball vs man?
- Do they call it out to teammates?
- Do they switch when ball moves?

### Game Connection
"In games, everyone needs to know: am I guarding the ball or a man? Call it out so your teammates know too!"

### Common Mistakes
- All defenders chasing the ball (remind: "Some have MAN!")
- Not calling it out (remind: "TALK! Say ball or man!")

## Ball-Help Sliding

**Phase:** Defense
**Objective:** Understand team defense - one player pressures ball, others help nearby
**Duration:** 15 minutes
**Equipment:** 1 goal, 1 ball, cones, pinnies
**Group size:** 8-12 players

### Setup
- Goal with crease marked
- 3 offensive players positioned around goal
- 3 defensive players
- Ball with offense

### I Do (3 min)
**Show the concept:**
- "In defense, we work as a TEAM!"
- Point to defender on ball: "He's on BALL - pressure!"
- Point to nearby defenders: "They are HELP - ready to help if needed!"
- "Watch: if ball gets past me, HELP player steps up!"
- Demonstrate a simple slide/help situation
- "See? Ball-Help-Help! We're all connected!"

**Key terms:**
- **"Ball-Help!"** - Identify your role
- **"Pick a guy!"** - Help players each have a man
- **"Help!"** - Call it when you slide to help

### We Do (6 min)
**Practice positions:**
1. Set up 3v3 with ball at top
2. Assign roles: "You're BALL. You two are HELP."
3. Walk through: "Ball defender pressure. Help defenders ready nearby."
4. Move the ball: "New ball! Who's on ball now? Who's help?"
5. Practice rotating as ball moves
6. Keep it slow and clear

**Coaching points:**
- Ball defender: tight pressure
- Help defenders: close enough to help, but guard your man
- Rotate as team when ball moves

### You Do (6 min)
**Live 3v3:**
- Offense tries to score
- Defense must communicate: "Ball! Help! Help!"
- Focus: are they working together?
- Coach calls out: "Who's ball? Who's help?"

**Success criteria:**
- Do they understand Ball-Help concept?
- Are they positioned correctly?
- Working as a team?

### Game Connection
"In games, defense is a TEAM job! One pressures ball, others help. That's how we protect the goal!"

### Common Mistakes
- All defenders far from their man (remind: "Help is close by!")
- Ball defender getting beat with no help (remind: "Help! Slide!")

## Hockey for Ground Balls

**Phase:** Defense
**Objective:** Use hockey stick move or kick to get ball out of scrums (no raking!)
**Duration:** 15 minutes
**Equipment:** 4-5 balls, cones for grid
**Group size:** 8-12 players

### Setup
- Small grid (10x10 yards)
- Multiple balls scattered on ground
- Players in pairs

### I Do (3 min)
**Show the concept:**
- Put ball on ground in traffic
- "When there's a scrum (bunch of players), we DON'T rake like this..." (show raking - bad)
- "We HOCKEY or KICK it out!" (demonstrate)
- Show hockey move: stick horizontal, push ball out
- Show kick: kick it away from traffic
- "Get it OUT of the scrum, then pick it up!"

**Key terms:**
- **"Hockey!"** - Push it out with stick
- **"Kick it!"** - Kick it away from trouble
- **"No raking!"** - Don't try to scoop in traffic

### We Do (6 min)
**Practice together:**
1. Create mini-scrums with 2-3 players
2. Ball on ground in middle
3. Coach: "HOCKEY! Get it out!"
4. Players use stick to push ball out of scrum
5. Once clear, they can pick it up
6. Repeat with different players

**Coaching points:**
- Don't reach in and try to scoop - you'll lose it!
- Get it to open space FIRST
- Then pick it up clean

### You Do (6 min)
**Scrum competition:**
- 2v2 or 3v3 ground ball battles
- Ball in middle, players must hockey/kick it out
- Point for clean pickup AFTER getting it out
- Reinforces the habit!

**Success criteria:**
- Are they using hockey/kick instead of raking?
- Do they get it to space first?

### Game Connection
"In games, when there's a pile of players, DON'T reach in! Hockey it out, then grab it!"

### Common Mistakes
- Reaching in to scoop (remind: "Hockey it out first!")
- Standing still (remind: "Push it! Kick it!")

## Body Between

**Phase:** Defense
**Objective:** Learn proper defensive positioning - your body between attacker and goal
**Duration:** 15 minutes
**Equipment:** 1 goal, 1 ball, cones
**Group size:** 8-12 players

### Setup
- Goal at one end
- Pairs: 1 offensive player, 1 defensive player
- Ball with offense

### I Do (3 min)
**Show the concept:**
- Stand next to offensive player with ball
- "This is WRONG - I'm next to him!" (show bad position)
- "This is RIGHT - my body is BETWEEN him and the goal!" (show good position)
- "See? He has to go through me to score!"
- Move around, always keeping body between attacker and goal

**Key terms:**
- **"Body between!"** - Between attacker and goal
- **"Protect the goal!"** - What we're doing

### We Do (6 min)
**Practice positioning:**
1. Pairs spread out around goal
2. Offensive player moves slowly with ball
3. Defensive player maintains "body between" position
4. Coach corrects positioning: "Get between him and the goal!"
5. Switch roles, repeat
6. Gradually increase speed

**Coaching points:**
- Always between attacker and goal
- Move your feet to stay in position
- Don't reach - use body position

### You Do (6 min)
**1v1 live:**
- Attacker tries to score
- Defender maintains body between position
- Focus: Is defender positioned correctly?
- Not worried about checks or steals, just POSITION

**Success criteria:**
- Do they understand body position?
- Can they maintain it while moving?

### Game Connection
"In games, good position beats speed! Keep your body between attacker and goal and they can't score!"

### Common Mistakes
- Standing next to attacker instead of between (reposition them)
- Reaching for ball instead of using body (remind: "Body between!")
