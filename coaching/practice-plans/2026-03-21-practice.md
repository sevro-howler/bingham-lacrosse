## Station Rotation (6:30-7:15)

3 groups rotating through 3 stations. 15 min each. Water break between rotations.

---

### Station 1: RIDE — "What is Riding?"

**Objective:** After we shoot, don't stand there — 2 attack and 1 middie pressure the clear and try to get the ball back!

**Setup:** Goal at one end. 2 attack + 1 middie (riders) vs goalie + 2 defense (clearing). In 3/4 lacrosse we play 2 attack — this is their job after a shot.
**Live players:** 6 (2 attack + 1 middie + goalie + 2 defense)

**I Do (3 min):**
- "We just shot on their goal and missed. Their goalie picks it up. Now what?"
- "Our 2 ATTACK and 1 MIDDIE stay and RIDE! That means CHASE THEM!"
- Demo: goalie gets ball, coach (as attack) sprints at them: "RIDE! RIDE!"
- "Attack — this is YOUR job! You're already down here, so PRESSURE the clear!"
- Show the WRONG way: stand and watch after shooting. "That's free for them!"
- "If we get it back, we shoot AGAIN!"

**We Do (6 min):**
1. Goalie starts with ball at goal, 2 defense nearby
2. 2 attack + 1 middie set up to ride
3. Coach: "Their goalie has it — what do we do?" → Players: "RIDE!"
4. Riders chase at half speed — attack pressure goalie/defense, middie covers middle
5. If they cause a turnover — celebrate!
6. If defense clears it past midfield — reset, try again
7. Rotate players through all positions

**You Do (6 min):**
- Full speed: 2 attack + 1 middie vs goalie + 2 defense
- Offense shoots, then immediately rides
- Defense tries to clear past midfield
- 20-30 second reps, rotate teams
- Keep score: 1 point for turnovers caused by riding

**Key words:** Ride, "Get it back!", "No easy pickups!"

---

### Station 2: DEFENSE — "Ball-Help"

**Objective:** On defense, know your job — are you on BALL (guarding the ball carrier) or HELP (ready to support)? When the ball moves, you SWITCH. Big rule: **1 defender per offensive player — no doubling up!**

**Setup:** Goal at one end. 3 offense (pinnies) with 1 ball. 2 defense (different pinnies). This is 3v2 around the goal.
**Live players:** 5 (3 offense + 2 defense)

**I Do (3 min):**
- Point to offensive player with ball: "He has the ball!"
- Point to one defender: "You're on BALL — guard him tight!" → defender says "BALL!"
- Point to other defender: "You're HELP — you're ready to jump in if ball gets past him!" → defender says "HELP!"
- Now pass the ball to a new offensive player
- "BALL MOVED! Watch: HELP becomes BALL (slide to the new ball carrier). Old BALL becomes HELP (recover and reposition)!"
- "See the switch? That's Ball-Help!"

**We Do (6 min):**
1. Set up 3v2 around the goal — offense spread out
2. Coach: "Who has ball?" (players point)
3. Assign: "You're BALL. You're HELP."
4. Ball defender pressures. Help defender stays ready nearby.
5. Offense passes to new player
6. Coach: "BALL MOVED! HELP — GO! You're BALL now! Old BALL — slide to HELP!"
7. Walk through the switch several times — SLOW
8. Emphasize: HELP slides to new ball carrier, old BALL repositions as HELP
9. **"1 defender per offensive player! Don't both go to the same guy!"**
10. Repeat. "BALL!" "HELP!" "SWITCH!" over and over

**You Do (6 min):**
- Offense passes the ball around the goal (slowly at first)
- On every pass, defenders must switch: Help → Ball, Ball → Help
- Defenders call it out: "BALL!" "HELP!" on every switch
- Coach stops play randomly: "Who's ball? Who's help? Are you in the right spot?"
- Speed up passing as they get it
- Focus: can they switch smoothly and reposition?

**Key words:** "Ball!", "Help!", "Switch!"

---

### Station 3: CLEAR — "Where Do You Go?"

**Objective:** Learn WHERE to move during a clear. Attack comes to midfield. Middies spread out — one runs the tightrope (along the sideline), one goes to the middle of the field. Long passes to move the ball.

**Setup:** Full half-field. Goal at one end. Mark 3 clearing positions with cones:
- **Cone 1 (Tightrope):** Along the sideline, near midfield — right on the boundary line
- **Cone 2 (Middle):** Center of field at midfield
- **Cone 3 (Attack):** Just past midfield on the offensive side

Goalie at goal. 2 defense near goal. 1 middie at tightrope. 1 middie at middle. 1 attack at midfield.
**Live players:** 6 (goalie + 2 defense + 2 middies + 1 attack)

**I Do (3 min):**
- Draw it on the whiteboard: "When we CLEAR, everyone has a JOB and a PLACE to go!"
- "Goalie gets the ball. Defense is near goal to help."
- "Middies — one of you runs the TIGHTROPE!" (run along sideline, trace the boundary) "Stay right on that line!"
- "Other middie — go to the MIDDLE of the field! Spread out!"
- "Attack — come BACK to midfield to give us an outlet!"
- Demo the passes: Goalie → Defense → Tightrope middie → Middle middie or Attack
- "See? LONG passes, everyone in their spot, ball moves up the field!"

**We Do (6 min):**
1. Set players in their clearing positions
2. Goalie starts with ball
3. Walk through: "Goalie to defense... defense look up... where's the tightrope? THERE! Long pass!"
4. Tightrope middie catches, looks for middle middie or attack at midfield
5. "CLEAR! Ball is out!"
6. Reset, rotate positions so everyone learns each spot
7. Emphasize: go to YOUR spot before the ball comes!

**You Do (6 min):**
- Full speed clears
- Goalie saves shot, yells "CLEAR!"
- Everyone runs to their position — tightrope, middle, attack at midfield
- Execute the clear with long passes
- Add 1-2 riders for pressure
- Celebrate successful clears past midfield!

**Key words:** Clear, "Tightrope!", "Middle!", "Get to your spot!"

## Final Drill — All Together (7:15-7:30)

### "Save, Clear, Ride"

Single-motion drill combining all 3 phases. One continuous rep, rotate, next group.

**Setup:** Goal at one end. Midfield line marked with cones.
**Live players:** ~7 per group (goalie + 2 defense + 1 middie clearing vs 2 riders + 1 middie riding)

**Each rep:**
1. Coach shoots on goal — goalie saves it
2. **CLEAR:** Goalie yells "CLEAR!" — 2 defenders spread to outlets, middie runs the tightrope. Goalie passes to an open player, they move the ball past midfield.
3. **RIDE:** 2 riders pressure the clear — chase, cut off passes, try to steal it before midfield
4. Ball past midfield = clearing team wins. Rider gets it = riding team wins.

**Run in 3 groups** (~10-11 players each). All 3 groups run the same drill simultaneously on different parts of the field.

**Rotation after each rep:**
1. **Clearers → Riders** (you just cleared, now try to stop the next clear)
2. **Riders → Sideline** (rest)
3. **Sideline → Clearing positions** (fresh legs, step into goalie/defense/middie spots)

Every player cycles: **Clear → Ride → Rest → Clear...**
They learn both sides and get a breather between reps.

**Coach calls out:** "CLEAR! Get to your spots!" → "RIDE! No easy pickups!" → "ROTATE!"

Keep it moving — one fluid rep, rotate, next rep.
