## Station Rotation (5:20-6:05)

3 groups rotating through 3 stations. 15 min each. Water break between rotations.

---

### Station 1: FACEOFF - "Man-Ball"

**Objective:** Understand what a faceoff is. Learn "Man-Ball" (who gets ball, who guards who).

**Setup:** 2 cones about 3 feet apart (the faceoff X). Ball between them. Split into two teams with pinnies.

**I Do (3 min):**
- "A faceoff happens at the start of each quarter and after every goal!"
- "Two players fight for the ball here" (point to X)
- Demo: go for ball, then point — "I got BALL! You guard that MAN!"

**We Do (6 min):**
1. Two players at the X, ball between them
2. Whistle → fight for the ball
3. STOP the drill when one gets it: "Who has BALL? Who has MAN?"
4. Rotate pairs, coach calls "MAN-BALL!" each time
5. Keep it slow and clear

**You Do (6 min):**
- Continuous faceoffs, rotating pairs
- After each one, players call out: "I got ball!" or "I got man!"
- Everyone gets 2-3 attempts
- Keep it competitive and high energy!

**Key words:** Man-Ball, "Ball!", "Man!"

---

### Station 2: TRANSITION - "Wheels"

**Objective:** Understand that transition = run fast from defense to offense.

**Setup:** 2 cones 40-50 yards apart (defensive end to offensive end). Balls at defensive cone.

**I Do (3 min):**
- "After we get the ball on defense, we TRANSITION to offense. That means RUN FAST!"
- Pick up ball at defensive cone, sprint full speed to offensive cone
- "This is WHEELS — no slowing down!"

**We Do (6 min):**
1. Players line up at defensive cone
2. First 3 grab balls
3. Whistle → sprint to offensive cone
4. Coach runs WITH them: "WHEELS! WHEELS! Keep going!"
5. Everyone gets 3-4 reps

**You Do (6 min):**
- Split into 2-3 relay teams
- Sprint with ball from one cone to the other, tag next teammate
- Teammates shout "WHEELS!" while runner goes
- First team done wins!

**Key words:** Wheels, Transition

---

### Station 3: OFFENSE - "Shooting Line"

**Objective:** Shoot from the line (12-15 yards), don't run all the way to the crease.

**Setup:** Cones in an arc 12-15 yards from goal (the "shooting line"). Pile of balls at the line. Run 2 lines if you have 2 goals.

**I Do (3 min):**
- "In offense, we shoot from HERE" (point to line)
- Demo the RIGHT way: run to the line, stop, shoot
- Demo the WRONG way: run all the way to the crease
- "See the difference? Shooting from the LINE is smarter!"

**We Do (6 min):**
1. Each player gets a ball
2. Walk to the shooting line (slow at first)
3. Stop at the line, set feet, shoot
4. Coach: "Stop at the line! Good!"
5. Repeat 3-4 times, gradually add speed

**You Do (6 min):**
- Shooting contest from the line
- 1 point for shooting FROM the line
- 0 points if you run past it
- Keep score, make it competitive!

**Key words:** Shooting Line, "Don't run to the crease!"

## Final Drill - All Together (6:05-6:15)

### 3v2 Fast Break

Combines all three phases learned today.

- 3 attackers vs 2 defenders
- Start with a faceoff at midfield
- Winner transitions with WHEELS
- Attack and shoot from the SHOOTING LINE
- Rotate groups, keep it fast and fun

**Coach calls out:** "Man-Ball!" → "Wheels!" → "Shooting Line!"
