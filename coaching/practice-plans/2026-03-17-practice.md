## Practice Schedule

### 5:00-5:10 — Warm-Up (10 min)
- Light jog around the field
- Dynamic stretching
- High knees, butt kicks, lunges
- Arm circles and stick stretches

### 5:10-5:25 — Stick Skills (15 min)
**Partner Passing Drill**
- Players pair up, 10 yards apart
- 50 right-hand passes/catches
- 50 left-hand passes/catches
- Focus on: soft hands, catching away from body, quick release

**Coaching Points:**
- Call for the ball early
- Give a target (stick head out front)
- Cushion the catch

### 5:25-5:45 — Ground Ball Drills (20 min)

#### Drill 1: Ground Ball Form (10 min)
- Players line up on end line
- Coach rolls out balls one at a time
- Players approach, scoop through, protect
- Focus on: low hips, through the ball, protect stick

**Coaching Points:**
- "Scoop through, don't rake"
- Two hands on stick for control
- Protect the stick with body after scoop

#### Drill 2: Ground Ball Hustle (10 min)
- 3 lines at midfield
- Coach rolls ball out, first player from each line sprints to get it
- Winner transitions to goal, others play defense
- Rotate lines

**Emphasis:** First to the ball wins — hustle!

### 5:45-6:05 — Small-Sided Games (20 min)

#### 3v3 Ground Ball to Goal
- 30x30 yard grid
- Coach rolls in ground ball
- Teams fight for possession
- Score on small goals or end zones

**Rules:**
- Must scoop ground ball cleanly to start possession
- 5-pass minimum before shooting (encourages movement)
- Rotate teams every 3-4 minutes

### 6:05-6:15 — Wrap-Up (10 min)

#### Team Huddle
- Review what went well Saturday
- Highlight improvements for Thursday
- Announcements:
  - Thursday practice: same time/place
  - Next games: Saturday (see schedule)
- High fives, "Go Bingham!"
