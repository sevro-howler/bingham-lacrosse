#### Station 1: TRANSITION DEFENSE — "Hold the Fort"

**Objective:** 1 defender slides up to BALL on the middie coming downhill. The 2nd defender drops off his man to HELP — reading where the middie is going to pass. Hold until the 3rd defender arrives and matches up.
**Live players:** 6 (1 middie + 2 attack on offense, 2 defense + 1 trailing defender)

**Setup:** Half field with goal. 2 attack set up near goal with 2 defenders on them. 1 middie at midfield with ball. 1 trailing defender at midfield (starts 5 seconds late).

**I Do (3 min):**
- "Their middie has the ball and he's coming downhill. What do we do?"
- "One D slides UP to the ball — you're on him! Call BALL!"
- "The other D drops off his man into HELP — you're reading where the middie wants to pass"
- "The middie sees you on him, he's going to pass to one of the attack. HELP defender — READ it and jump the pass!"
- Show the WRONG way: both defenders stay on their attack, middie walks in and scores
- Show the RIGHT way: D1 slides to ball, D2 drops to help, reads the pass
- "3rd defender sprints back and picks up whoever is open — now we're matched up!"

**We Do (6 min):**
1. 2 attack set up near goal, 2 defenders on them
2. Middie starts at midfield with ball, jogs downhill (half speed)
3. Coach: "He's coming! Who's got BALL?" → one defender slides up to middie
4. Coach: "Who's HELP?" → other defender drops off, reads the passing lane
5. Middie tries to pass to attack — help defender jumps the lane
6. Coach counts: "5... 4... 3... 2... 1... 3RD DEFENDER IN!"
7. 3rd defender sprints in, matches up with the open man
8. Walk through 3-4 times, then increase speed

**You Do (6 min):**
- Full speed: middie carries downhill
- D1 slides to ball, D2 drops to help and reads
- Middie can pass to either attack or try to score — help defender must read it
- 3rd defender releases 5 seconds after whistle
- Defense gets a point for stopping the score or jumping a pass
- Offense gets a point for scoring before 3rd defender arrives
- Rotate everyone through all positions

**Key words:** "Ball!", "Help!", "Read it!", "EVEN!"

---

#### Station 2: OFFENSE — "Pineapple & Peanut Butter"

**Objective:** Learn 2 offensive sets. Pineapple = diamond, X carries and passes to open wing cutting. Peanut Butter = wide spread, ball carrier goes 1v1 with space.
**Live players:** 4-5 (offense only, no defense — learn the movement first)

**Setup:** Half field with goal. 4 offensive players. Cones marking the 4 positions.

**I Do (3 min):**
- Set up Pineapple (diamond): X at bottom, 2 attack on wings, middie at top
- "Watch: X carries to the right. What does the left wing do? He CUTS to the open side!"
- Demo the X carry → open wing cut → pass → shot
- Now set up Peanut Butter (wide): 2 attack high, 2 middies low, spread wide
- "Watch: ball carrier picks a side and goes 1v1. Everyone else STAYS HOME!"
- Demo the iso dodge → shot

**We Do (6 min):**
- **Pineapple (3 min):**
  1. Players in diamond positions
  2. Coach walks them through: "X carry right... left wing where are you going? CUT!"
  3. X passes to cutting wing → wing shoots
  4. Reset. Rotate positions. Repeat 4-5 times.
  5. "Call it out: PINEAPPLE!" → everyone knows the play

- **Peanut Butter (3 min):**
  1. Players in wide spread positions
  2. Coach: "Who has the ball? Pick a side. Everyone else STAY."
  3. Ball carrier dodges 1v1 → shoots
  4. Reset. Rotate who carries. Repeat 4-5 times.
  5. "Call it out: PEANUT BUTTER!" → everyone spreads wide

**You Do (6 min):**
- Alternate reps: coach calls "PINEAPPLE!" or "PEANUT BUTTER!"
- Players set up the right formation and run the play
- No defense yet — just get the movement clean
- Add a goalie if available for shooting
- Focus: do they know where to go when they hear the call?

**Key words:** "Pineapple!" (diamond, X to open wing), "Peanut Butter!" (spread wide, iso)

---

#### Station 3: DEFENSE — "Trap!"

**Objective:** When they have a strong player, we double team him. Learn the positioning: where do the 2 trapping defenders go, and where does the 3rd defender rotate to cover the open man.
**Live players:** 5 (2 offense + 3 defense)

**Setup:** Half field near goal. 2 offensive players — 1 is "their best player" (ball carrier), 1 is an outlet. 3 defenders — 2 trappers, 1 reading/rotating.

**I Do (3 min):**
- Give ball to one player near the sideline: "Pretend this is their best guy. He's been scoring all game."
- "When coach yells TRAP, two defenders form a FUNNEL — a V shape!"
- Demo with another defender — show the V:
  - **Defender 1 (Body):** "I play his BODY. I get between him and the goal. I cut off his dodge."
  - **Defender 2 (Stick):** "I play his STICK. I get on his stick side. I take away his pass."
  - "See the V? We're funneling him in. There's no seam to split!"
- Show a FLAT line: attacker splits right through. "That's WORSE than 1v1! The V prevents that!"
- Show the slider announcing: "COMING LEFT!" → body defender pushes attacker left
- Point to 3rd defender: "You — who's open? Go cover him!"

**Trap Cues — Building the Funnel:**
1. **"V shape!"** — Not a flat wall. Angle in like a funnel. He gets squeezed with no seam to split.
2. **"Body and stick!"** — One plays his body (cut off dodge), one plays his stick (take away pass). Know your role.
3. **"Coming left/right!"** — Slider announces which side they're coming from. On-ball defender pushes attacker INTO the slider.
4. **"Push him into me!"** — The body defender IS the sideline. Push the attacker toward the slider. Near the sideline, the line does this for you. In the open field, YOU are the sideline.
5. **"Squeeze, don't rush!"** — Close space under control. Rushing in creates the split. Chop your feet, get low, close slowly.
5. **"No reaching!"** — Don't lunge at the ball. That opens the gap. Stay in your stance, stay patient.
6. **"Eyes on hips!"** — Watch his hips, not his stick. His hips tell you where he's going.
7. **"He's dead!"** — When the trap is set: maintain pressure. He can't dodge, he can't pass clean.
8. **"Find a man!"** — If ball gets out of the trap, everyone resets and finds a man immediately.

**We Do (6 min):**
1. Ball carrier stands with ball near the sideline — he's "their best player"
2. 1 outlet player stands 10 yards away
3. Coach: "The sideline is our third defender — he can't escape that way!"
4. Coach yells: "TRAP!"
5. Slider calls: "COMING RIGHT!" → approaches from the right in a V angle
6. Body defender pushes attacker toward the slider
7. They form the V — **"Can he split you? Try it!"**
8. Ball carrier tries to split or dodge out — if he gets through, tighten the V
9. If the V holds: "He's dead! 3rd defender — who's open? Go!"
10. If ball gets passed out: "FIND A MAN! Reset!"
11. Repeat, rotate who plays each role

**You Do (6 min):**
- Live reps: ball carrier near sideline tries to escape, split, or pass out
- Coach calls "TRAP!" — slider announces side, defenders form V, 3rd reads
- **Progression:**
  1. First 2 min: 2v1 (no outlet) — just practice the V shape and closing
  2. Next 2 min: 2v1 + outlet — now ball carrier can pass, 3rd defender reads
  3. Final 2 min: Full live — ball carrier tries everything, defense traps and recovers
- Keep score: defense point for turnover, offense point for clean escape or pass
- Rotate through all positions

**Key words:** "Trap!", "V shape!", "Body and stick!", "Coming left/right!", "He's dead!", "Find a man!"
